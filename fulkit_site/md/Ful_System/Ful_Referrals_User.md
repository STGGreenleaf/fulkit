# Get Fülkit — Referral Program

Every friend who joins Fülkit earns you Fül. Stack enough and your subscription pays for itself. Keep going and Fülkit pays you.

---

## How It Works

1. **Share your referral link** — find it in Settings → Referrals
2. **Friends sign up** — they get a 14-day free trial
3. **You earn Fül** — when their first payment clears, you start earning 100 Fül/month per active referral
4. **Fül offsets your subscription** — or converts to cash at higher tiers

That's it. One friend = 100 Fül/month. No limits. No catch. If they cancel, the credit pauses. If they come back, it resumes.

## How Fül Works

- **100 Fül = $1** — that's the exchange rate
- Fül offsets your subscription first (like a credit)
- Once your subscription is covered, excess Fül stacks as balance
- At 25+ referrals (Builder tier), excess converts to cash payouts via Stripe

## Cash Payouts

- Unlock at **Builder** tier (25+ active referrals)
- Connect your Stripe account in Settings → Billing
- Payouts process on the **1st of each month**
- **$10 minimum** — amounts under $10 roll over to the next month
- Fül-to-cash rate: 100 Fül = $1, adjustable quarterly with 30 days notice

---

## What Counts

- Only **active paying** referrals earn you Fül — trial users don't count until their first payment clears
- If a referral cancels, their credit pauses. Your tier adjusts automatically.
- If they come back, the credit resumes
- You can track all of this in Settings → Referrals — your tree, your earnings, your tier

## FAQ

**Can I refer unlimited people?**
Yes. No cap. Every referral is net positive.

**What if my referral cancels?**
Your Fül drops by that referral's contribution. If it drops you below a tier, your tier adjusts. If they rejoin, it comes back.

**When do I get paid?**
1st of each month, automatically. $10 minimum per payout — anything less rolls over.

**Do I need to be on a paid plan to refer?**
No. Free users can refer too. Fül offsets any future subscription.

**How do I set up payouts?**
Settings → Billing → Connect Stripe. Fülkit handles the rest.
