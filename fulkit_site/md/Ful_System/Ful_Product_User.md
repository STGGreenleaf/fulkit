# What is Fülkit?

Fülkit is a thinking partner that knows everything you've saved, talks back, and never makes you start from zero.

Other AI tools forget you between conversations. Fülkit doesn't. You open it, you talk, it already knows what you're working on.

---

## What You Get

### Chat
AI-powered conversation that knows your notes, your preferences, and what you're working on. Ask "what was that idea I had about pricing?" and it finds it instantly. Ask "draft an email based on my meeting notes" and it pulls the context and writes it. No catching up. No re-explaining.

### The Hum
Voice mode. Talk to a floating orb — no transcript on screen, no inner editor. Ramble for 5 minutes, close it, and find perfectly organized notes and action items later. Morning brain dumps, walking brainstorms, hands-free thinking.

### Whispers
Proactive suggestions that drift in naturally. "You mentioned reworking the pricing page — your notes on loss aversion might help." Not notifications. Not alerts. Just a friend who noticed something useful. If you don't act on it, it fades. No guilt.

### Actions
Your to-do list writes itself. Drop a document and Fülkit extracts deadlines, follow-ups, and decisions. Every interaction teaches it your rhythm — push tasks to Friday often enough and it learns that's when you handle follow-ups.

### Vault
Your personal knowledge base. Three storage options — local-only, encrypted sync, or Fülkit-managed. Your data, your choice, your control. Import notes from anywhere.

### Fabric
Audio visualization and music integration. Connect your Spotify and see your listening in a new way.

## Integrations

Fülkit connects to the tools you already use:
- **GitHub** — access repos, files, and code context in chat
- **Spotify** — music integration via Fabric
- **Numbrly** — cost management (vendors, components, builds)
- **TrueGauge** — analytics integration
- **Square** — POS and inventory management
- **Shopify** — e-commerce integration
- **Toast** — restaurant POS
- **Trello** — project boards

Connect any integration in Settings → Sources. Each one gives Fülkit more context to work with.

## Privacy & Data

- Your notes are yours. Fülkit never trains on your data.
- Three vault modes: keep everything local, encrypt and sync, or let Fülkit manage it.
- BYOK users route API calls through their own key — Fülkit never sees the traffic.
- You can export all your data anytime from Settings.
