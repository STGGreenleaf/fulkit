# The Bestie Test — Fülkit Onboarding

> How Fülkit builds a brain from nothing. 20 questions. Real-time brain building.
> See also: [buildnotes.md](buildnotes.md) for product spec, [brain-restructure.md in ChappieBrain] for vault structure these answers map to.

---

## Philosophy
This is not an interrogation. It's a conversation with a purpose. Every question exists because the answer directly shapes how Fülkit helps the user. The user KNOWS why each question matters — transparency builds trust.

## UX Design
- **Not a form.** A conversation. Fülkit asks, user answers, brain builds in real-time.
- **Multiple choice with "or tell me" option.** Fast, pointed, never limiting.
- **Follow-up questions preferred.** Short answer → follow-up to deepen. Not 20 questions in a row.
- **Grouped by purpose.** Each group explains WHY these questions matter.
- **Skip anything.** "Pass" is always an option. No pressure.
- **Progress feel.** User sees their brain building: "Got it — I just created your Goals file."

## The Flow

### PHASE 1: The Basics (2 min)
*"Let me get the fundamentals so I can talk to you like a human, not a robot."*

**Q1:** What should I call you?
→ *Creates your identity file. Everything starts with a name.*
`[text input]`

**Q2:** Where are you based?
→ *Helps me with time zones, local recommendations, and context.*
`[text input]`

**Q3:** What do you do?
`[Employee] [Self-employed] [Student] [Between things] [Retired] [Tell me]`
→ *Shapes whether I think in terms of bosses, clients, customers, or professors.*
→ Follow-up if self-employed: "What's the business?"

**Q4:** What's your day-to-day like?
`[Desk job / computer all day] [On my feet / physical work] [Mix of both] [It changes constantly] [Tell me]`
→ *This determines when and how I check in with you.*

**Q5:** Are you here to organize your life, your work, or both?
`[Personal life] [Work / career] [Both — it's all connected] [I don't know yet]`
→ *Decides whether I build you a personal brain, a work brain, or the full thing.*

---

### PHASE 2: Your People (2 min)
*"I want to know who matters to you so I never ask you to spell their name twice."*

**Q6:** Who are the 2-3 most important people in your life?
→ *I'll remember them, their names, their role in your life. If you want me to remind you about birthdays — I need this.*
`[text input — names and relationship]`
→ Follow-up per person: "When's their birthday? (skip if you don't know)"

**Q7:** Anyone I should know about at work?
`[Boss / manager] [Business partner] [Team members] [Clients] [Nobody — I work alone] [Tell me]`
→ *Helps me give advice that accounts for real dynamics, not hypothetical ones.*

---

### PHASE 3: What's on Your Plate (3 min)
*"I can't help if I don't know what you're carrying. This is where it gets useful."*

**Q8:** What's the #1 thing you're trying to get done right now?
→ *This becomes your first action item. I'll check in on it.*
`[text input]`

**Q9:** What keeps falling through the cracks?
`[Emails / follow-ups] [Health stuff] [Money / bills] [Personal errands] [Projects I start and don't finish] [Tell me]`
→ *This tells me where to focus my whispers. If you forget follow-ups, I'll nudge you.*

**Q10:** How do you feel about your current level of organization?
`[Total chaos] [I have a system but it's messy] [Pretty organized] [Obsessively organized] [I don't think about it]`
→ *Tells me how much structure to impose vs how much to stay out of the way.*

---

### PHASE 4: How You Want Help (2 min)
*"This calibrates how I talk to you and how often I check in."*

**Q11:** How do you want me to communicate?
`[Short and direct — don't waste my time] [Warm and conversational] [Challenge me — push back when I'm wrong] [Just be helpful, I'll figure out the vibe]`
→ *Directly sets my tone. This is the most important UX question.*

**Q12:** How often should I check in with suggestions?
`[A couple times a day is great] [Once a day max] [Only when I ask] [Surprise me — I'll tell you if it's too much]`
→ *Sets your whisper frequency from day one.*

**Q13:** What topics should I help with?
`[Work / productivity] [Health / fitness] [Food / meal planning] [Finance / budgeting] [Personal growth] [All of it] [Tell me]`
→ *Scopes what I pay attention to. "Only food stuff during the day" starts here.*

**Q14:** How do you capture ideas right now?
`[Notes app on my phone] [I don't — they just disappear] [Paper / journal] [Voice memos] [A specific app] [Tell me]`
→ *Tells me whether to push voice mode, quick capture, or meet you where you already are.*

---

### PHASE 5: Quick Hits — Your Preferences (1 min)
*"Rapid fire. These shape your experience behind the scenes."*

**Q15:** Morning person or night owl?
`[Early bird] [Night owl] [Depends on the day]`
→ *Determines when whispers arrive.*

**Q16:** Do you want daily sport scores, news, weather?
`[Sports — tell me which teams] [News — tell me which topics] [Weather is useful] [None of that — just my stuff] [All of it]`
→ *Tells me if I should be a morning briefing or stay focused on your brain.*
→ Follow-up if sports: "Which teams?"
→ Follow-up if news: "What topics?"

**Q17:** One thing you wish an app could do for you that none of them do?
→ *This is the gold. This tells me what magic moment to create for YOU specifically.*
`[text input]`

---

### PHASE 6: The Deep Stuff — Optional (2 min)
*"Only if you want to go here. This makes me significantly better but it's personal. Skip any or all."*

**Q18:** What's one goal you have that you haven't told many people?
→ *I'll quietly track this and check in when the time is right. No pressure.*
`[text input or skip]`

**Q19:** What stresses you out most right now?
`[Money] [Health] [Relationships] [Work] [Time] [Everything] [Nothing major] [Tell me]`
→ *I'll be careful around these topics. Won't add pressure — just awareness.*

**Q20:** What does a really good day look like for you?
→ *This is my north star for how to help. If a good day includes a workout and family dinner, I'll nudge toward that.*
`[text input or skip]`

## What Happens as They Answer

Real-time brain building. After each phase:

- **Phase 1 done:** "Got it — I created your profile and know when to check in."
- **Phase 2 done:** "Your people are saved. I'll remember [name]'s birthday."
- **Phase 3 done:** "I set up your first action item and I know where to focus."
- **Phase 4 done:** "Your Fülkit is calibrated. I know how to talk to you."
- **Phase 5 done:** "Preferences set. Whispers are ready."
- **Phase 6 done:** "I know you a little deeper now. I'll be thoughtful with it."

**Total:** ~20 questions, 10-12 minutes for the full path. 5 minutes for the fast path (Phase 1 + 3 only).

---

## Quick Onboard

Phase 1 (Q1-Q5) + Q8 + Q11 + Q12. That's 8 questions, 3 minutes.

Enough for a functioning bestie with a name, a context, a first task, and a communication style. Everything else builds through use.

| Phase | What it creates |
|:---|:---|
| 1: Basics | `01-PERSONAL/About-[Name].md`, timezone, work type |
| 2: People | `01-PERSONAL/People/[Name].md` per person, Important-Dates.md |
| 3: Plate | First action item, whisper focus areas |
| 4: How you want help | Tone calibration, whisper frequency, topic scope |
| 5: Preferences | Morning/night timing, briefing content, feature priorities |
| 6: Deep stuff | Goals.md, stress-aware whisper calibration, "good day" north star |
